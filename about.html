About Decision Women Network

Empowering Nigerian women to reach their full potential through education, economic opportunities, and advocacy

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Decision Women Network began as a small community initiative in Surulere, Lagos, with the goal of helping women in our neighborhood gain skills to support their families.

What started as vocational training classes for 20 women has grown into a nationwide movement impacting thousands of women annually across Nigeria.

Today, we operate skills acquisition centers, run educational programs, provide microloans, and advocate for policies that promote gender equality at all levels of society.

Our Mission

To empower Nigerian women through education, skills development, and economic opportunities that enable them to become self-reliant and active contributors to their communities.

Our Vision

A Nigeria where every woman has equal access to education, economic opportunities, and the ability to participate fully in all aspects of society without discrimination.

Our Core Values

These principles guide everything we do at Decision Women Network

Empowerment

We believe in equipping women with the tools they need to transform their own lives.

Education

We see education as the foundation for personal and societal transformation.

Integrity

We are committed to transparency and accountability in all our operations.

Compassion

We approach our work with empathy and respect for the women we serve.
