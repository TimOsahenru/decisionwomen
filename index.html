Our Story

Empowering Nigerian Women Since 2010

Wise Women Network was founded in Lagos to address gender inequality and empower women across Nigeria. We believe that when women are educated and economically empowered, entire communities thrive.

Our Mission

To create opportunities for Nigerian women through education, skills development, and advocacy.
